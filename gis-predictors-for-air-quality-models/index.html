0.1 Details of all candidate predictor variables

We conducted a targeted literature review to identify potential candidate predictor variables. We then identified datasets from both local collections (e.g. the Australian Government Public Sector Mapping Agency) and international sources (e.g. NASA). The datasets are listed in Table 1. We collected the relevant data in formats that could be combined in a Geographical Information System (GIS) database. All GIS data were extracted for the monitor locations and a grid of points for estimation approximately every 100 metres.
